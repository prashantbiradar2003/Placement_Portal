About Our Placement Portal

Shri Dharmasthala Manjunatheshwara College of Engineering & Technology, Dharwad was established in the year 1979 under the flagship of SDME Society and has grown into one of the nationally acclaimed premier engineering institutions in the country.

The institution has been consistently rated high, ranking within 50, among the top private engineering colleges in the country.

The college has a dedicated placement cell that works towards ensuring maximum employability of its students. Our unified portal aims to optimize the placement process, providing a streamlined and efficient platform for both students and recruiters. By combining our resources and expertise, we offer a wider range of job opportunities to our students, while also giving recruiters access to a larger pool of talented and highly skilled individuals.

Many leading companies from different sectors such as IT, Manufacturing, Core engineering, etc., regularly visit the campus for recruitment. Some of the notable recruiters include companies like Cisco, Mercedes-Benz, Accenture, Bosch, Juniper Networks and many more.
